Dragon Age: Inquisition

Dragon Age: Inquisition is an epic role-playing game set in the fictional fantasy world of Thedas where players lead the Inquisition to save a fantasy world from chaos, with complex choices and rich storytelling shaping their journey.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a sprawling open-world RPG where players assume the role of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter, on a quest to find his adopted daughter while navigating a medieval fantasy world filled with rich storytelling and challenging choices.

Overwatch

Overwatch is known for its fast-paced gameplay, colorful characters, and emphasis on teamwork and strategy. The game has a strong focus on online multiplayer, and it has become a popular esports title since its release.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The Legend of Zelda series follows the adventures of Link, a hero who embarks on quests in the magical land of Hyrule, solving puzzles, battling monsters, and ultimately, striving to rescue Princess Zelda and thwart the evil plans of Ganon.

God of War (2018)

God of War is an action-adventure game that chronicles the journey of Kratos, a former Greek god, and his son Atreus as they navigate the realms of Norse mythology, battling powerful foes and uncovering a deeply emotional and mythic narrative.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is an action-adventure game set in feudal Japan, featuring a shinobi named Wolf who engages in intense, precision-based combat to rescue his kidnapped lord and confront supernatural adversaries in a challenging, unforgiving world.

The Last of Us Part II

The Last of Us Part II is a gripping action-adventure game that follows Ellie, a young woman, as she navigates a post-apocalyptic world, seeking revenge and survival while grappling with complex moral dilemmas, in a story-driven narrative filled with emotional depth and stunning visuals.

It Takes Two

It Takes Two is a cooperative action-adventure game where players take on the roles of two characters, Cody and May, who are transformed into dolls, embarking on a whimsical and heartfelt journey to mend their fractured relationship through inventive puzzles and cooperative gameplay.

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is an upcoming action RPG developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game is set in a dark fantasy world, and players will explore a vast open world, engage in challenging combat, and uncover a rich lore as they seek to break the Elden Ring's shattering curse.
